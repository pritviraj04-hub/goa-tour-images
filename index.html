Places to Visit

Carefully selected destinations that combine education with exploration

Latin Quarter · Art · Architecture

Day 1 · Evening

Heritage Walk — Fontainhas

A guided walk through Goa's iconic Latin Quarter — colourful Portuguese-era homes, chapels and cobblestone lanes come alive at dusk.

History · Architecture · Colonial Heritage

Day 2 · Morning

Reis Magos Fort

A 16th-century Portuguese fort perched above the Mandovi River, offering stunning views and a window into Goa's colonial past.

Marine Ecology · Coastal Science

Day 2 · Afternoon

Bambolim Beach

A serene beach ideal for coastal ecology studies — students explore marine ecosystems, sand dune formation and conservation practices.

Botany · Agriculture · Environment

Day 3 · Morning

Tropical Spice Plantation

An immersive agricultural experience where students learn about spice cultivation, tropical botany and sustainable farming.
